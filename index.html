Story behind the lens

Hi, i'm Oguche Emmanuel Happiness, a Lagos-based photographer and visual poet.

Photography, to me, is the gentle art of listening with light. I am drawn to the trembling anticipation before a bride steps forward, the burst of laughter like sudden sunlight, the unspoken glances between lovers, and the tender in-between silences where true emotion lives.

With every frame, I seek not perfection, but truth — the raw poetry of emotion and the beauty of imperfection. Whether documenting vibrant Nigerian weddings, intimate elopements, or soulful family moments, my camera becomes a bridge between the present and forever.

I've had the honor of capturing over 50 celebrations across Lagos, Abuja, and beyond. Every photography is a love letter written in time. I would be honored to create with you.
